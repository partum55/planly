How it works

Five steps. Zero effort.

From casual chatter to concrete plans — Planly handles the entire pipeline automatically.

01
Observe
Captures messages from Telegram or screenshots of any chat app
02
Reason
LLM parses intent, extracts people, times, and locations
03
Plan
Determines actions: events, venue search, time suggestions
04
Act
Executes via Google Calendar, Places, Yelp, and Maps
05
Respond
Returns action cards, calendar pickers, and confirmations
Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Multi-platform input

Screenshot + OCR for any chat app on your desktop, or native Telegram bot integration for group chats.

Natural language

Understands casual messages. Extracts plans, times, locations, and participants from how people actually talk.

Google Calendar

Creates events directly from conversations. Date, time, location, and participants — all auto-filled.

Venue suggestions

Finds restaurants and places through Google Places and Yelp. Get ratings, distance, and links in one response.

Multi-turn conversation

Ask follow-ups, refine the plan, change details. Planly keeps context across the entire conversation.

Cross-platform

Desktop app for Linux, Windows, and macOS. Telegram bot for group chats. Use what fits your workflow.

Architecture

Built to be modular.
